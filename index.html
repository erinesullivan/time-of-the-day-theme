Time of the Day Theme

This is a theme that changes its color palette throughout the day. These color transitions happen four times a day:

  • Morning (6:00am - 11:59am)
  • Afternoon (12:00pm - 5:59pm)
  • Evening (6:00pm - 11:59pm)
  • Night (12:00am - 5:59am)
